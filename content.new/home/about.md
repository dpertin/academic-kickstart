# Biography

After receiving a Master of Engineering degree in Systems and Networks from the
University of Nantes in 2012, I started a [Ph.D]
(https://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/tel-01720038v1) in computer science
about the designing of fault-tolerant, low-cost and high-performance coding
techniques for distributed storage systems. With funding from [Rozo Systems]
(https://www.rozosystems.com/), and under the supervision of Nicolas Normand
and Benoît Parrein, I defended my thesis on 2016.

Since then, I have been working on the [Discovery Initiative]
(http://beyondtheclouds.github.io/) as a postdoctoral researcher, at the
[STACK](http://stack.inria.fr/) team of Inria and LS2N. My research deals with
distributed application deployment and reconfiguration on dynamic
and massively-distributed infrastructures (e.g. Fog and Edge computing) .
